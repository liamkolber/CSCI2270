Check this box to view your Digital ID Card and reset release approvals before logging into the service. Learn More...

By leaving this box unchecked, you will log directly into the IT service.

You only need to check this box if you wish to view your Digital ID Card and/or make changes to it before you log into the IT service. Once you are done reviewing and/or editing your Digital ID card, you may click “Confirm” to submit your changes and log into the IT service.

Digital Id Card

The first time you access this website through this login page, you will be presented with a Digital ID Card listing the attributes that will be released. You will be asked to release the information. If you choose to deny the release, you will be unable to access the website you are trying to log into. At anytime, you can check the box next to View and release my Digital ID Card. Upon checking and logging in you will have the opportunity to view your Digital ID Card again and be prompted to provide your consent when you login to all websites that utlize Federated Identity Service.

Trouble signing in? We can help.

To ensure you end your session with Federated Identity Service, you will need to quit your web browser when you are finished. Leaving your browser open may make you more vulnerable to another user gaining access through your account.

Note: Due to the nature of this authentication page loading dynamically per service, DO NOT bookmark the URL in your browser's address bar. Instead, bookmark the service URL (e.g. https://voicethread.colorado.edu or https://qualtrics.colorado.edu).
